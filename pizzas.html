Featured Recipe

Pizza Dough

  • 4 C. bread flour
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 envelope instant dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 C. water, 110 degrees F
  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil, plus 2 tsp.

Combine the bread flour, sugar, yeast and kosher salt in a stand mixer and combine. Add the water and 2 tablespoons of the oil and beat until the dough forms into a ball. Scrape the dough onto a lightly floured surface and gently knead into a smooth, firm ball.Grease a large bowl with the remaining olive oil, add the dough, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and put it in a warm area to let it double in size, about 1 hour. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and divide it into 2 equal pieces. Cover each with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let them rest for 10 minutes.
