Featured Recipe

Grilled Cheese Roll-Ups

  • 6 slices of Bread
  • 6 American Cheese Singles
  • 1 tbsp Butter

Roll each slice of bread to flatten. Trim crust off of bread. Place cheese slice on bread and roll up. Heat skillet over medium low and melt butter. Place rollups in pan seam side down. Cook until brown turning frequently throughout cooking. Serve immediately.
